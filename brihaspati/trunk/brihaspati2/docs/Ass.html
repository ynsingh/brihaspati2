How to Post Assignment

  1 . Topic Name - In "Topic Name", Instructor can assign the Topic name for particular courses, Topic name can't be repeated for particular courses. Availability is being checked after assigning the Topic name.

   2. Due Date - In "Due Date", The instructor assigns due date for their assignments. Students have to submit their assignments within due date.
   3. Instruction - The instructor can give instructions for his assignments.
   4. Max Marks - In this section, the instructor assigns maximum Grade for their assignments.By default is 10
   5. Attach file - The instructor can attach any assignment's file which has to be only with the .txt, .pdf, .html, .doc, .odt, .docx, .pptx, .zip, .ppt, .odx, .odp, .ods, .zip extension.
   Note : Availability option is optional, that means the instructor can check it or not too.
