Student Attendance Management

Student Attendance Management module will be developing for multiple Institute for online attendancesubmission and it's monitoring by Teachers. Students also have access to view their attendance. In this the teachers engagingdifferent courses are required to submit the attendance of the students present in their class regularly. Teachers will submit their attendance through this Student Attendance Attendance Management System.

"Student Attendance Management" has basically three role given below.

Institute Admin

Instructor

Student/Guardian

    Institute Admin

    Institute Admin will access the Brihaspati LMS through their username and password. Upon submitting the user name and password the Institute Admin go to his Home Page. There he get the Student Attendance Module link. From these the Institute Admin can see and update attendance of student by selecting of any course associated with Instructor or by selecting of any month or between two date of months.

    Instructor

    Instructor will access the Brihaspati LMS through their username and password. Upon submitting the user name and password the teacher go to his CourseHome. There he get the Student Attendance Module link. From these the teacher can submit daily attendance of student and and also see the attendance report of student by selecting of any month or between any date of month. Teacher will get all the list of students for the assigned Course. In this list all the students have been marked present by default therefore teacher will have to unclick the students not present in their class. Submit the attendance after unclicking the absent students.

    Student / Guardian

    Students / Guardian can also look at their attendance / attendance of their wards by selecting of any month or between any date of month.
