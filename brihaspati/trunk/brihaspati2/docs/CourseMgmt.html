Course Management

Course Management System is an information system that is used by Instructor to post information on the web without having to comprehend other computer languages. Course Management System gives an instructor a set of tools and a framework, which makes it easy to create online course content, and also afterward aiding the teaching of the course along with student interaction

    Course Days

    TIn the Course Days box, instructor will mension the course days in form of 135(Mon,Wed,Fri), 246(Tues,Thus,Sat), 123456(Mon to Sat every day) and custom (mension no of days and order by himself).

    Marks/Grading Policy

    In this, instructor will mension the brake-up of marks in course (for eg. quiz marks, assignment marks endsem marks midsem marks).

    Instruction

    In this instructor will mension the rules/comments/relevent information about the course.

    Course Syllabus

    Syllabus will be uploaded by this option. The syllabus file should be of .txt, .pdf or .htm format.
