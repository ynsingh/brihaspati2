This page shows the list of all Instructor's including (primary and secondry) in a course. Light Green color of row shows the main Instructor of Course

Main Instructor can add Secondry Instructor in their course as well as he can change their permission. when permission of secondry instructor will be changed it will work as a primary Instructor in perticular course. when permission is granted from secondry to primary Instructor it shows in the Light Pink color row.

Only Institute admin and primary instructor can remove secondry Instructor.
